Bangkok Guangdong Cemetery (sBangkokBangRakGuangdongCemetery)

The Bangkok Guangdong Cemetery (曼谷廣東墳場) is located at Silom Road, at the backside of the Cantonese school, close to the Hakka and the Hokkien graveyard. The cemetery is used as a commercial parking lot. On the premises of the graveyard there is also an Earthgod temple, which cannot be entered though. Not only people from Guangtong are buried here. It might be the case that the tombs not from Guangdong date from a time when the cemetery was not yet specifically dedicated to the Guangdong community. Likewise one can find Qing-Dynasty Hokkien tombs on the Hakka graveyard. A more detailed study of this site and the other sites is thus required, looking especially at the earliest tombs.
In 2023 or before, a 5m high metal fence has been set up to prevent people from entering the cemetery and from viewing what is going on in the cemetery. People entereing the cemetery are threatened with violence. Cars are still parked in the first part of the cemetery. They seem to belong to a special clientel as they can enter and leave freely. Cameras have been installed to control who enters the cemetery.
