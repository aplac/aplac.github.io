The Bangkok Guangdong Cemetery (曼谷廣東墳場) is located at Silom Road, at the backside of the Cantonese school, close to the Hakka and the Hokkien graveyard. The cemetery is used as a commercial parking lot. On the premises of the graveyard there is also an Earthgod temple, which cannot be entered though.Not only people from Guangtong are buried here. A more detailed study of this site is thus required.
