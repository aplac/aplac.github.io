The generals of Lintou are the result of a transformation of Lintou from a village protected by four Earthgod shrines to a village protected by five generals. Maybe as a result of this transformation, we find today each shrine of a general occupied by Tudi Gong. In addition, seven generals instead of the usual five generals protect the village. We currently ignore, when this transformation took place and whether the position of the former Earthgod shrines influenced the choice of the position of the generals. Much more research needs to be done. The central general faces the road to Huxi, watching neither outward nor inward, but from south to north along the eastern border of the village.
