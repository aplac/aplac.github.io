Thakbong

Oliver Streiter

The Thakbong project is an open-ended project, which in its current stage can best be described as a digital endeavor to document, archive and research epigraphy in the Taiwan Strait and beyond.

The entire project is divided into various sub-projects which reflect the interest of researchers at a given time. The sub-projects can also been understood as simplified approach to the search of sites and items. Currently the following projects are distinguished:
