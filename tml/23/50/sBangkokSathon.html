Hypotheses:
We hypothesize, that at the time of the erection of these sites, around 1900, the foreign cemeteries, temples and churches were outside the city of Bangkok.
We hypothesize, that the area of the foreign cemeteries, temples and churches seemed of little use to the Thai people. Probably, because this area was easily and frequently flooded.
