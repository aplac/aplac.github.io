Amsterdam is the capital of the Netherlands and its most populous city. It serves as the cultural center of the country, preserving a rich cultural and historical heritage. Through its political and economic significance, Amsterdam\'s cultural heritage not only reflects the history of the Netherlands but also that of Europe and its colonial past. The heritage of the city doesn\'t hide the painful aspects of its history, such as the deportation and murder of approximately 100,000 Jews by the German Nazi regime and by those who served and collaborated with it. Much of the work of Thakbong targets the documentation of Jewish gravesites and other sites of importance for the history of Jews in the Netherlands.
