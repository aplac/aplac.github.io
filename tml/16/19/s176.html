A Bodhi Tree close to the Christian and Parsi cemetery at Silom Road. The site is filled with discarded religious objects. It also hosts a a san phra phum (ศาลพระภูมิ, shrine of Phra Phum) and a san chao thi (ศาลเจ้าที่).
Two shrines for tutelary deities on Silom Road.
