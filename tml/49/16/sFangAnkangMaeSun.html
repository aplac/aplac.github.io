Ankang (sFangAnkangMaeSun)

Ankang/Mae Sun is a settlement in the Fang District in the northeast of Chiang Mai. It is inhabited mainly by the offspring of Kuomintag soldiers and a Muslim community. The village has a small Muslim cemetery, a mosque, a Chinese language school and a San Shen Miao.
