澳門伊斯蘭清真寺及墳場 (sMacauMosqueAndCemetery)

The Macau Mosque and Cemetery, also known as the Macau Islamic Cemetery or Cemitério Islâmico de Macau in Portuguese, is a significant religious and cultural site located in the city of Macau, China. Despite its historical and cultural importance the site is modest in size and architectural features. The most emblematic element of the complex is the gate by which visitors can enter from the Ramal dos Mouros. Following Islamic traditions, the graveyard is mostest, if not humble, and thus contrasts with the style and maintenance of the Parsi, Christian and Chinese cemeteries in Macao. The mosque has been renovated in 2007 and a new, larger mosque is intended to be built.
Names: 澳門伊斯蘭清真寺及墳場, Macao Mosque and Cemetery, Macau Mosque and Cemetery, Mesquita e Cemitério de Macau
Types: territory, cemetery
