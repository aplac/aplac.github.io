Xinpi (sPindongXinpiJiangong)

Xinpi is a village of the Xinpi township in Pindong. The other villages of the Xinpi township compresise Datie (打鐵村), Jiangong (建功村), Jihu (箕湖村), Nanfeng (南豐村), Wanlong (萬隆村), Xiangtan (餉潭村) and Xinbei (新埤村).
