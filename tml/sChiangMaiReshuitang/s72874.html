Description:
The tomb of Kuomintang General Li is located in the center of Reshuitang village on a walled area. Before the tomb stretches a vast open plaza, spacious enough to accommodate thousands of visitors or potentially an assembled army formation paying homage to the general. The memorial itself presents a curious duality – part unfinished construction site, part grandiose monument, clearly designed to immortalize General Li as a national hero. This architectural tension reflects the complex historical narrative surrounding his legacy.
