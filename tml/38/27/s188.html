This cemetery was once located at the center of the village. With the prolongation of Zhongzheng Road to the north of Jiazhan Road, the graveyard was removed. Before being removed, the cemetery had some overgrown and abandoned parts, and some better maintained parts. My last visits to this cemetery were in 2012 and 2014 and it is about that time that the cemetery has been removed.
