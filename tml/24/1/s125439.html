fieldwork 13:59:53 15:29:35 2010-07-01 2010-05-20 : Sandy Ko-jui YAN: Meifang:
The first graveyard we visited on Penghu. Because of its hilly terrain the site had probably the best fengshui in the Magong area. In 2010 still the largest and oldest graveyard in Magong, this site has now been removed. Where once the ancestors of Magong have found their resting place, boring residential buildings and guesthouses for summer tourists have been built. The cultural loss is immense. Not only are the spiritual definition of spaces bluntly overwritten. This graveyard was also exceptional for the age of the tombstone inscriptions, some from the Qianlong and Jiaqing aera, the particular architecture of some tombs, as well as less unqiue forms of tombstones and tombstone wings, documenting traditions of immigrants before they got unified in a generalized Penghu-style.
