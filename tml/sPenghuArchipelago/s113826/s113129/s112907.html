With a system of seven generals, a system of Wufu Qiansui, a protective stone invoking Fude Zhengshen and a certain number of shigandang, Lintou is a spiritually well protected village. Unfortunately most shigandang are heavily withered so that we can assume, that many more shigandang might actually have been installed in Lintou in the past, which, without some historic documents, will be difficult to identify in future field work.
