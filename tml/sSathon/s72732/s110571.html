Name: ศาลเจ้าแม่กวนอิม, Bangkok Guanshiyin Pusa Gong, Bangkok Guanyin Temple, 觀音菩薩宮
Type: Taoist temple, 廟, temple
ID: s110571
Description:
This is a small shrine on 798-2 Mangkon Road, at the west-side of the road, beside the Wat Kanma Tuyaram. Due to the limited terrain, the temple is much broader than deep. Having no front wall, the shrine is protected by an iron rolling gate that can be closed at night. Wolfgang Franke visited this shrine.
