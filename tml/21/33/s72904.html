Cemetery in Church Ruin (s72904)

A church graveyard in a now desagregated church. The location of the tombs is inside the former church building, yet only a few walls remain in that part of the buidling. The tombs are arranged perpendicular to the orientation of the church. While the church is mainly directed towards east, the tombs are oriented either to the north or the south.
