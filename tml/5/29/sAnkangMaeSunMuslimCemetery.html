Names: Ankang, Mae Sun,
Types: settlement

(sAnkangMaeSunMuslimCemetery)

The Angkang Muslim cemetery lies in the north-east of the village. The road to the cemetery is not well maintained, the cemetery even less. Only a few tombs can be found there, a few are unmarked. The community of the mosque of Ankang consists of Muslim of various ethnicities. Similar can be assumed for the cemetery and only the tombs of Chinese Muslims are marked with a tombstone.
Types: cemetery, Muslim cemetery
