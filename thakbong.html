Thakbong -- 讀墓

A Research and Documentation Projet of
A P L A C
亞太語言文化研究中心
Research Center for Asia-Pacific Languages and Cultures,
College of Humanities and Social Sciences,
National University of Kaohsiung, Taiwan

The Thakbong project is an open-ended project, which in its current stage can best be described as a digital endeavor to document, archive and research epigraphy in the Taiwan Strait and beyond. Read more ...

The data are hosted on GitHub in the ThakbongMarkupLanguage repository, where they can be freely downloaded and allow for collaborative elaboration and extension.
