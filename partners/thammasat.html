APLAC means ... collaboration with the Pridi Banomyong Internation Collegage at the University of Thammasat in Bangkok

In January 2024 APLAC and the Pridi Banomyong Internatinoal College at the University of Thammasat in Bangkok signed an MOU, promoting joined reserach and joined publication activities. In addition, the two partners start a research project on the Hokkien Graveyard in Bangkok.
